We are working for cleanliness of the oceanes and the nature

Our oceans and nature need someone to stand up for them and if nobody wants to do it we will. Read more about our work here.

Things we have to deal with on a daily basis and how we try to prevent it

People polluting the water on purpose.

Many people have no regard for keeping anything clean much less the oceans from plastics so we strive to explain to them that this is not the way every time we see something like this.

Factories throwing their plastic waste in the oceans.

Many factories have nothing to do with their waste and they throw it in the oceans polluting it without a drop of remorse.

Ocean animals trapped in plastics.

The animals that inhabit the ocean often happen to be entrapped in plastic residue and we try to help them whatever way we can.
